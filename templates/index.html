Workflow

How DiagnosTenAI Works

From disease selection to downloadable PDF report — three steps, seconds to complete.

Step 01

Select Disease

Browse the 10 disease categories on the dashboard and tap Run Prediction on the condition you want to assess.

Step 02

Enter Clinical Data

Fill in the patient parameters — lab values, vitals, age, BMI — shown dynamically from the trained model's feature columns.

Step 03

Get AI Report

Receive instant results: prediction outcome, confidence score, risk level, clinical recommendations, and a one‑click PDF report.
